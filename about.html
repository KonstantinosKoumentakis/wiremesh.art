Wire Mesh Art

by Konstantinos Koumentakis

HOME WORK ABOUT CONTACT
Konstantinos, a London-based artist from Greece, merges the realms of technology and art through his novel technique. Drawing upon his studies at UCL and experience as a data scientist in the luxury fashion industry, he brings a unique blend of technical expertise and creative expression to his work.

Moving beyond the conventional confines of wire mesh, typically viewed as an industrial material, Konstantinos creates distinctive pieces of art. Each piece stands as a testament to his hands-on approach, where he meticulously manipulates the material to craft captivating images that come to life through the interplay of shadow and light.
